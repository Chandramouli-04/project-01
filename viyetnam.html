VIETNAM

Vietnam is a beautiful Southeast Asian country known for its natural beauty, rich culture, and historic sites. From vibrant cities to quiet villages and breathtaking landscapes.

Top Places to Visit in Vietnam

1.Hanoi

Hanoi – Capital of Culture and Tradition
Hanoi is Vietnam’s historic capital, founded over 1,000 years ago on the banks of the Red River. Known as the “Paris of the East,” it uniquely blends ancient temples, French-colonial architecture, charming lakes, and a vibrant street life




2. Ha Long Bay

Ha Long Bay, located in northeastern Vietnam, is one of the country’s most iconic and scenic destinations. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for its emerald waters and thousands of limestone islands topped with rainforests.




3.Sapa

Sapa is a charming town in the Lào Cai Province, nestled in the Hoàng Liên Son Mountains of northern Vietnam. It is known for its breathtaking rice terraces, cool climate, and vibrant ethnic minority cultures.
