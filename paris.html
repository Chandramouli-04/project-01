PARIS

Description:

Paris, the capital of France, is one of the most iconic cities in the world—famous for itsart, architecture, fashion, cuisine, and romantic charm . Located on the banks of the River Seine, Paris is often called "La Ville Lumière" (The City of Light), both for its role in the Age of Enlightenment and its early adoption of street lighting.

List of must-visit tourist places in Paris, ideal for both first-timers and returning travelers

1.Louvre Museum (Musée du Louvre)

Highlight: Home to the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo
Tip: Arrive early to avoid long lines; closed on Tuesdays



2.Notre-Dame Cathedral

-->Notre-Dame is a famous Gothic cathedral located on the Île de la Cité in Paris. Built between the 12th and 14th centuries, it’s known for its twin towers, rose windows, flying buttresses, and gargoyles. It is a masterpiece of medieval architecture and a symbol of French heritage. In April 2019, a fire severely damaged the roof and spire. Restoration is ongoing, with plans to reopen by late 2024. The cathedral was also made world-famous by Victor Hugo's novel The Hunchback of Notre-Dame.



3. Eiffel Tower

Highlight: Iconic landmark of Paris
Things to Do: Go up to the top for panoramic views, dine at the 58 Tour Eiffel restaurant
